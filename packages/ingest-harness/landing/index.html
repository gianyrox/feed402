Every dataset answers /raw, /query, /insight on the same wire shape. Every response carries a mandatory citation envelope per feed402/0.2. Every row is renderable on a globe with a time slider scrubbing 3000 BCE → 2030.

How an agent pays for a query

# 1. hit the endpoint without payment — get the 402 challenge
$ curl https://feed402.agfarms.dev/p/world-history/query?bbox=-15,35,40,60
{"error":"payment_required","price_usd":0.005,"chain":"base-sepolia",...}

# 2. sign an x402 payment payload, replay with X-PAYMENT header
$ curl -H "x-payment: $(x402-sign '$0.005' base-sepolia 0xYourAddr)" \
       https://feed402.agfarms.dev/p/world-history/query?bbox=-15,35,40,60
{"data":{"rows":[...]}, "citation":{...}, "receipt":{"tier":"query","price_usd":0.005,"tx":"0x..."}}

What's in here

  • Pharmaceuticals — every FDA drug approval w/ sponsor HQ + approval date.
  • World history — battles, treaties, revolutions, plagues, earthquakes (Wikidata, BCE-aware).
  • US history — battles, laws, SCOTUS cases, civil rights events.
  • Constitutions — every national charter w/ adoption year + capital.
  • Math & physics — birthplaces of every named mathematician + physicist 1500–2010.
  • Banking — World Bank financial-stability indicators × 158 countries × 25 years.
  • Banking history — 938 banks, central banks, exchanges by founding date + HQ.
  • World politics — heads of state, treaties, summits, coups, referendums (1945–present).
  • Medical history — epidemics, pandemics, outbreaks, famines, diseases.
  • Per-nation — all of the above, sharded by ISO country (93% polygon-exact).
